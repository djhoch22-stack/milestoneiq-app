## Talking points & objection handlers
- **"We already use MaxPreps/Hudl."** Great — keep them. RaftersIQ imports straight from their exports and turns them into a permanent, school-owned record book + Hall of Fame they don't give you.
- **"We don't have time to enter all this."** You don't. Upload a season's PDF or CSV and the AI reads the players and stats. One season at a time, a few minutes each.
- **"What about our old records?"** Import historical seasons the same way, or type them into the Excel template. Imports merge, so nothing gets overwritten.
- **"Is our athletes' data safe?"** Per-school, role-based access for your AD and coaches. Nothing is public unless you publish it.
- **"What's it cost?"** From $15/mo, 7-day free trial, no card to start, cancel anytime. Whole-school plans for multi-sport ADs.
- **The hook for ADs:** every team gets a public record book + Hall of Fame page — perfect for banquets, signing days, alumni outreach, and your athletics website.
