# RaftersIQ — First 10 Schools Plan

## The goal (read this first)
The first 10 is **not** about 10 sales. It's about landing **3–5 design partners** who actually
use RaftersIQ this season, plus **1–2 testimonials/references** you can point the next 50 schools
to. Optimize for *replies and real usage*, not revenue. Revenue follows references.

## Your unfair advantage
You're an athletic director. You're not a vendor cold-pitching — you're a **peer who built a tool
for your own program and it works.** That sentence converts. Lead with it every single time.

## How to pick your 10 (concentric circles — warm beats cold ~10:1)
Open your **league/conference schedule** and your **contacts**, and pick:
- **Tier 1 — 5 schools you have a *person* at.** ADs/coaches you actually know: league opponents,
  people from CHSAA meetings, your coaching network, fellow private/Christian schools. → personal **text or call**.
- **Tier 2 — 3 schools one step away.** In your conference or nearby, reachable via a mutual
  contact or a "fellow AD" intro. → **email**, name the connection.
- **Tier 3 — 2 cold but targeted.** Programs with real tradition/history (lots of records = big
  "wow"), your size. → **cold email**, pure learning + maybe a win.

**Best-fit profile:** small-to-mid programs with **history/tradition** (more records to show off),
where you know someone, in the sports you nail (basketball, baseball, softball, soccer, football).
Skip the giant programs with big budgets for now.

## The offer that makes the first 10 say yes
Kill all friction — **do the work for them:**
> "I'll set up your record book and import your last couple seasons **for free** — you just look at
> the finished thing. 7-day trial, no card."
- White-glove import = highest conversion, and you're already fast at MaxPreps/GameChanger imports.
- Optional **founding-school perk:** a promo code for a locked-in discount or a few extra free
  months + a "Founding School" nod. First movers should feel special.

## Week-1 cadence (~20–30 min/day)
- **Mon** — finalize your 10 (table below). Text/message all **Tier 1 (5)**.
- **Tue** — email **Tier 2 (3)**, referencing the mutual connection.
- **Wed** — for anyone who said "sure," **build their record book + send the link** (white-glove).
- **Thu** — **follow up** Tier 1/2 non-responders. Send **Tier 3 (2)** cold.
- **Fri** — demo calls / set up trials for the interested; jot down every objection you heard.
- **Goal by Friday:** 3–5 record books built, 1–2 "yes I'll use this," 1 quote you can reuse.

## When they say yes (white-glove flow)
1. Get their team's season export (MaxPreps PDF or Hudl/GameChanger CSV) — or pull a public one.
2. Create their program → import 1–2 seasons → add each season's record (W–L) so wins auto-assign.
3. Send the live link: *"Here's [School]'s record book — what do you think?"*
4. Ask the magic question: **"Who else should have this?"** → that's your referral engine (they get
   a free month, the new school a 14-day trial).

## Remember
- **Warm beats cold ~10:1** — spend your energy on Tier 1.
- **Show, don't tell** — the live record-book link does the selling.
- **Make it effortless** — "I'll build it for you" beats "sign up here."
- **Always end with "who else?"** — references compound.
